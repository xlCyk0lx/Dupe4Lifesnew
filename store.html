FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions

How long do ranks last?

All ranks are permanent! Once you purchase a rank, you'll keep it forever on our server.

How do I use the /dupe command?

Simply hold the item you want to duplicate in your main hand and type /dupe followed by a number (up to your rank's maximum) in the chat. For example, "/dupe 5" will duplicate the item 5 times if your rank allows it.

Can I upgrade my rank later?

Yes! You can upgrade your rank at any time and only pay the difference between your current rank and the new one. Contact an admin on Discord for assistance.

How soon will I receive my rank after purchase?

Ranks are applied instantly after a successful purchase! You'll receive your perks immediately and can start using them right away.
