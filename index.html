About Our Server

DUPE4LIFES is a Minecraft server created 5 years ago - that's crazy to think about! We thank all our members for sticking with us through this amazing journey.

Our server is a Lifesteal Dupe server - what does that mean? It's a unique gameplay experience that combines Lifesteal mechanics with Duplication commands like /dupe.

We were one of the first Lifesteal Dupe servers ever created, inspired by DupeKits but taking the concept to a whole new level!
